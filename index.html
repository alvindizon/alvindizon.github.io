GHL Launcher

This app is a replacement to the stock Launcher app that comes with a PAX A920 device. Aside from allowing users to add favorite apps, the GHL Launcher app acts as a bridge between other apps in the terminal and external data sources such as electronic cash registers (ECRs) allowing apps to receive and send data to the ECR via the GHL Launcher. This two-way communication is accomplished via a library. This app also sends card data to other apps whenever a card is inserted/swiped during idle operation.
